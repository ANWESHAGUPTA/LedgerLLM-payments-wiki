# Visa Dispute Process

## Summary
Visa's dispute resolution system handles transaction reversals when cardholders contest charges. The process involves the cardholder, issuing bank, acquiring bank, and merchant working through a structured workflow to resolve payment disputes.

## Details
Disputes refer to the reversal of transaction value (in whole or in part) by the card issuer to the acquirer, and usually by the merchant bank to the merchant. The process follows a clear sequence:

### Step 1: Cardholder initiates dispute
The cardholder contacts their issuing bank to dispute a transaction they don't recognize or believe is incorrect.

### Step 2: Merchant locates transaction receipt
Once notified of a dispute, merchants must locate the original transaction receipt and any supporting documentation.

### Step 3: Evidence submission to acquirer
Merchants send copies of transaction receipts and supporting evidence to their acquirer/processor for dispute response.
