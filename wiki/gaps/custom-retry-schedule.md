# Custom Retry Schedule

## Status
This concept is mentioned in sources but not explained in enough depth for a full article. Needs dedicated sources.

## What we know so far
Stripe allows merchants to override Smart Retries and create custom retry rules. You can configure up to three retries, each with specific timing after the previous attempt.
