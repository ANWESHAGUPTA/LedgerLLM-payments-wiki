# First-Party Misuse

## Status
This concept is mentioned in sources but not explained in enough depth for a full article. Needs dedicated sources.

## What we know so far
First-party misuse is Visa's official term for what's commonly called friendly fraud - when cardholders dispute legitimate transactions. Visa estimates roughly 75% of chargebacks may fall into this category.
