# Visa Arbitration

## Status
This concept is mentioned in sources but not explained in enough depth for a full article. Needs dedicated sources.

## What we know so far
Arbitration is the final stage in Visa's chargeback process when parties can't reach a decision. Either party can escalate within ten days, but it's costly with hundreds of dollars in fees for the losing party.
